ABOUT US


Precision Plastics Packaging Co. formerly known as Precision Plastics Printing Co. was originated in 1963. It was acquired by the current management in 1991 to expand and reach the level of excellence. Our 120,000 square foot manufacturing facility, located in Anaheim, California is equipped with all the necessary equipment to help in accomplishing the best results. Due to our high capacity manufacturing unit; we have the ability to print over 600,000 bottles per day. We operate 3 shifts per day to service your needs and overcome the challenging delivery schedules.


Our primary goal at PPPC is to provide you with the excellent quality and service. We focus on each and every slight detail to create the finest product. Our commitment to providing the best quality and service has enabled us to become one of the prominent manufacturers of plastic bottles. We at PPPC provide a pleasing experience to all of our customers by providing a complete package of service from designing the product to arranging the freight.

WHY US?


Precision Plastics Packaging Co. is a leading manufacturer and decorator of high quality plastic bottles. Since 1963, we have been providing our clients with highest quality of services and products.

We offer Injection Blow, Extrusion Blow and Stretch Blow molding and a wide array of bottles, which includes High Density Polyethylene, Polyethylene Terephthalate and Polypropylene.


The wide range of plastic bottles is offered in variety of different shapes and sizes with short lead times. Simultaneously, PPPC has the capability of printing any sort of bottle.

As a Screen Printer, we have many years of experience and the necessary equipment to attain the best results. We can also help in building a custom mold and creating the choice of bottle that perfectly satisfy your requirements.

WHAT WE DO


Extrusion Blow Molding

Extrusion Blow Molding is usually less expensive which is one reason it is considered great for small production runs. It requires low investment to set up and high rate of production. Mostly used for large containers with wide-ranging array of shapes, offset necks and molded handles.


Injection Blow Molding

Injection Blow Molding is considered great, since it delivers an enhanced orientation of the bottle with smooth surfaces and precise bottle weight. IBM also helps in attaining the most critical of the neck sizes with uniformity. It works best in creating the most complex shapes with ease.


Screen Printing

The process of Screen Printing commences with the given artwork that is followed by tooling. Tooling is necessary for the proper registration of the product. Once the tooling and artwork has been determined, product is prepared to be silk-screened. Lastly, the product is packaged and PPPC can help in transporting your product at a minimized cost.
